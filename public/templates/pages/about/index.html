About Ballot Box

Ballot Box features d3js powered pie charts with interactable legends to include or disclude options. A registered user can manage their poll by editing options, removing all votes, or deleting the poll. All polls are shareable.
Both registered and non-registered users can vote one time per Ballot Box. Registered users may also add their own option to the Ballot Box. Registered users can also change their votes if they change their minds.
About this project
